The charlie lodge is situated along Nairobi Nakuru highway with spectacular views of Lake Nakuru and Lake Elementaita, this quiet lodge in Nakuru kenya will have you relaxed and rested in no time. Offering luxury accomodation for both local and international guests, the charlie lodge has become a home away for many discerning guests.
